Speakers

TechConnect hosts hundreds of the top minds in technology. Pick your poison - whether it's social media, fasion, cloud computing, or just about anything else, we've got one of the industry leaders on our stages. The amount of information you can gather from these folks is astounding so make sure you take notes (and even as a technology conference we don't judge those who use old-fashioned paper and pen for the job). TechConnect's speakers cover all the topics you want to hear about on multiple stages and in every conceivable format - keynote addresses, fireside chats, and riveting (and sometimes contentious) panel discussions.
